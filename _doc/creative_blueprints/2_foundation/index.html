The Problem

Pharmacies running blind on inventory and prescription records

Expired stock reaching patients

No proactive tracking means drugs expire on shelves — unnoticed until it is too late.

Prescription errors go undetected

Without a central record, duplicates and dosage conflicts slip through at dispensing.

Regulatory audits become a scramble

Scattered spreadsheets and paper logs make compliance evidence impossible to produce quickly.

Silent financial losses from wastage

Expired inventory written off with no visibility into what slipped through the cracks.

The Solution

One platform for inventory integrity and prescription safety

Proactive expiry alerts at 30, 15, and 7 days

Act before any stock becomes a liability — automated and always on.

Digital prescription log with accuracy checks

Every prescription recorded; duplicates and dosage anomalies flagged before dispensing.

Single searchable inventory register

Stock levels, batch numbers, and expiry status in one place — no spreadsheets alongside.

Audit-ready records, always exportable

Every action logged; compliance reports available for any inspection, at any time.

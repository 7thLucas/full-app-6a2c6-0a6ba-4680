What it does

Core Features

Pharmacy Owner Pharmacist Dispensing Staff
📦
Drug Inventory Management

Track stock levels, batch numbers, and drug categories in one searchable register. Add, update, and reorder with ease.

📅
Expiry Date Tracking

Colour-coded status per item — Safe, Expiring Soon, or Expired — with batch-level detail at a glance.

🔔
Proactive Expiry Alerts

Automated warnings at 30, 15, and 7 days before expiry — surfaced on the dashboard before stock becomes a liability.

📋
Prescription Records

Digital log of every prescription: patient, drug, dosage, prescribing doctor, and dispense date. Searchable by patient or drug.

✅
Prescription Accuracy Checks

Flags duplicate prescriptions and dosage anomalies against existing records before dispensing is confirmed.

📊
Dashboard & Reports

At-a-glance inventory health and prescription accuracy metrics — exportable anytime for regulatory compliance audits.

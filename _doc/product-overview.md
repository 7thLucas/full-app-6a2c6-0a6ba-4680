# Product Overview — PharmaTrack *(working title)*

## What it is
PharmaTrack is an operational management app built for pharmacy owners and their staff. It provides a single, reliable system to track drug inventory, monitor expiry dates, and maintain accurate prescription records — so expired stock never reaches a patient and prescription errors are caught before dispensing.

## Users
- **Primary**: Pharmacy owners (independent and small-chain pharmacies)
- **Secondary**: Pharmacists and dispensing counter staff

## Core Problem
Pharmacies running on manual processes or scattered spreadsheets face two high-stakes risks:
1. **Expired stock reaching patients** — no proactive tracking means drugs expire on the shelf unnoticed until it is too late.
2. **Prescription errors** — without a centralised record system, prescriptions are filled inaccurately or dispensed twice, creating patient safety and legal liability.

A third downstream consequence: **regulatory non-compliance** — scattered paper logs make it impossible to produce clean audit evidence under pressure.

## Solution
A focused, web-based operational tool that:
- Tracks all drug inventory with expiry dates and batch numbers
- Fires proactive expiry alerts (30-, 15-, 7-day warnings) before stock becomes a liability
- Maintains a digital prescription log with patient history and accuracy checks at point of dispensing
- Keeps every action logged and exportable for regulatory audits — always

## Core Features
1. **Drug Inventory Management** — Add, update, and track drug stock levels; search by name, batch, or category.
2. **Expiry Date Tracking** — Per-item expiry monitoring with colour-coded status: Safe / Expiring Soon / Expired. Batch-level detail included.
3. **Expiry Alerts** — Automated in-app warnings at 30, 15, and 7 days for drugs nearing expiry, surfaced on the dashboard.
4. **Prescription Records** — Digital log of all prescriptions: patient name, drug, dosage, prescribing doctor, date dispensed. Searchable by patient or drug.
5. **Prescription Accuracy Checks** — Flags duplicate prescriptions and dosage anomalies against the existing record before final dispensing is confirmed.
6. **Dashboard & Reports** — At-a-glance inventory health, expiry summary, and prescription accuracy metrics. Exportable reports for compliance audits.

## Positioning
A focused compliance and accuracy tool for independent and small-chain pharmacy operations. Not a POS system, not a billing tool — purpose-built for inventory integrity and prescription safety.

## Brand & Tone
Professional, clinical, trustworthy. Calm confidence — the same qualities a good pharmacist projects. Clean, minimal UI with clear colour signals (green / amber / red for stock status).

## Strategic Principles
- **Prevent first** — surface expiry risks and prescription conflicts before they become incidents.
- **Single source of truth** — one system for inventory and prescriptions; no dual-entry, no spreadsheets alongside.
- **Audit-ready by default** — every action logged; exportable records available at any time.
